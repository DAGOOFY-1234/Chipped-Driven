# Chipped & Driven - Resource Matrix

## Tier 1: Primitive Materials (Jungle Biome)
Basic resources gathered by hand or with simple stone tools.

- **Flint Shards**
	- **Source:** Exposed rocky outcrops.
	- **Component Crafting:** Chipped Gear Teeth, Basic Axe Blades.
- **Jungle Vines**
	- **Source:** Hanging canopy vegetation.
	- **Component Crafting:** Tension Cables, Pulley Belts.
- **Prehistoric Sap**
	- **Source:** Giant amber-producing trees.
	- **Component Crafting:** Airtight Gaskets, Pipe Sealant.

## Tier 2: Industrial Components (Volcanic Biome)
Advanced components forged inside the Mobile Steam Haven's boiler station.

- **Obsidian Shards**
	- **Source:** Hardened lava flows.
	- **Component Crafting:** High-Durability Flywheels, Reinforced Drills.
- **Volcanic Sulfur**
	- **Source:** Bubbling hydrothermal vents.
	- **Component Crafting:** Boiler Fuel (increases heat generation speed).
- **Hollowed Redwood**
	- **Source:** Ancient petrified forests.
	- **Component Crafting:** Steam Conduit Pipes, Valve Casings.

## Tier 3: Apex Monster Trophies (Glacial Biome)
Rare structural elements harvested from hunting massive predators.

- **Mammoth Tusk**
	- **Source:** Elite Woolly Mammoth hunts.
	- **Component Crafting:** High-Pressure Pistons, Base Frame Structural Pillars.
- **Scurry-Fur Armor**
	- **Source:** Burrowing frost-reptiles.
	- **Component Crafting:** Thermal Insulation Wraps (prevents pipes from freezing).
