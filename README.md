# Chipped-Driven

## The Mobile Steam Haven
- **The Chassis:** A multi-layered platform made of massive redwood logs tied together with heavy jungle vines.
- **The Legs:** Four to six mechanical limbs crafted from the thick leg bones of Mammoths, driven by primitive sliding pistons.
- **The Core Boiler:** A giant, clay-reinforced volcanic stone kiln situated in the center of the base that burns sulfur and wood to boil water.
- **The Pipe Network:** A maze of hollowed-out bamboo and logs wrapped in animal fat to seal the steam pressure and route it to your crafting stations.

## Craftable Survival Gear
- **Pneumatic Bone-Spear:** A heavy spear connected via a flexible vine-hose to a leather steam-bladder worn on your back. It fires with enough force to punch through dinosaur hide.
- **Kinetic Flint-Axe:** An axe fitted with a tiny internal piston that fires right upon impact, multiplying your swinging force to shatter rocks and chop trees instantly.
- **Steam-Jet Grappling Hook:** A tool that launches a sharp bone hook using high-pressure steam, allowing you to quickly scale cliffs or escape giant predators.

## The Final Sanctuary
- **Geyser Caps:** Permanent stone domes built directly over natural steam vents to power your new village safely without burning fuel.
- **Automated Defense Turrets:** Stationary ballistas powered by steam-driven stone flywheels that automatically shoot harpoons at approaching monsters.
- **The Terraformer Core:** A massive, complex machine made of legendary geological pieces that purifies the toxic air and regulates the valley's temperature, creating a permanent safe haven for your tribe.
